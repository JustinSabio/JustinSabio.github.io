“We cast Siddhartha in the role of Issen for our project ‘Mizari Loves Company’. Since then, he has done excellent work that has been delivered in a very timely manner, well before our deadlines. We couldn’t be happier with his work. In addition, from his audition lines to his finished work, he has perfectly emulated the essence of Issen, where others didn’t quite seem to ‘get’ the character.”
